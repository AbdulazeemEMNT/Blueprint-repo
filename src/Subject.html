Academic Levels

Creche & Nursery

Early childhood education focusing on foundational skills, social development, and Islamic values. Play-based learning for ages 1-5.

Primary School

Basic education covering core subjects including Mathematics, English, Islamic Studies, and other foundational subjects for holistic development.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3)

Foundation years covering all core subjects, sciences, humanities, and introductory vocational studies. Students build fundamental knowledge across all subject areas.

Senior Secondary (SSS 1-3)

Specialized education with students choosing between Science, Commercial, or Arts tracks. Focused preparation for WAEC and NECO examinations.
