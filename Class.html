Available Classes

From early childhood to college preparation, we offer comprehensive Islamic education at every stage of your child's development.

Daycare (6 months - 2 years)

Our Daycare program provides a secure and nurturing environment where infants and toddlers receive personalized attention and care. Blueprint Schools combines early childhood development practices with Islamic values to support cognitive, emotional, and physical growth.

Through structured routines, sensory activities, and gentle guidance, children begin to develop social interaction skills and a sense of independence within a faith-centered atmosphere.

Nursery (2 - 5 years)

The Nursery program at Blueprint Schools offers a solid foundation for early learning through structured, play-based activities. Our curriculum blends Quran recitation, Arabic introduction, and essential Islamic teachings with early literacy, numeracy, and creative exploration.

We create an engaging environment where curiosity and confidence are nurtured, ensuring every child is prepared academically, socially, and spiritually for the next stage of learning.

Primary (6 - 11 years)

Our Primary School delivers a comprehensive academic program that balances traditional subjects within an Islamic framework. We emphasize Quranic studies, Arabic, and moral education alongside core subjects like Mathematics, Sciences, and Languages.

Students develop critical thinking, communication, and leadership skills through classroom learning, extracurricular activities, and continuous moral guidance — forming a solid foundation for lifelong learning.

College (12 - 18 years)

Blueprint College provides advanced secondary education designed to prepare students for university and global opportunities. The program combines rigorous academic study with a strong Islamic framework to produce intellectually capable and morally upright individuals.

Our students are encouraged to think critically, lead with purpose, and uphold values of integrity, discipline, and excellence. At Blueprint College, we cultivate future leaders equipped with both knowledge and character.
